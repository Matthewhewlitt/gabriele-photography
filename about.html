Our Story

Why Gabriele Photography

Big box stores sell cameras. We help you use them.

Most photography retailers push product and move on. You leave with gear but no real guidance — and prints that don't do justice to your work.

At Gabriele Photography, that's never been how we operate. John founded this shop in 1989, inspired by his uncle Gianfranco's camera store in Italy — a place where passion for the craft was inseparable from the service. That's the model we've followed ever since.

John and his team match you with the right equipment, teach you how to use it, and produce prints that actually look the way the moment felt.

From first-time buyers to semi-professional shooters — we've done this since 1989.

Expert Guidance

Advice you can trust

Our staff know cameras because they use them. Every recommendation comes from experience, not a commission sheet.

Fair Prices

No surprises at the counter

New and used gear priced honestly. We'd rather earn a repeat customer than a one-time margin.

35+ Years

A fixture in Collingwood

We've watched the Georgian Bay region grow and change. Through it all, we've been at 133 Hurontario — same shop, same values.
