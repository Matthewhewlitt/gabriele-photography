Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an appointment to come in?

No. Walk-ins are welcome anytime during business hours. Passport photos, printing drop-offs, and gear browsing are all available on the spot — no booking required.

Can I sell or trade in my old camera equipment?

Yes — bring in your used gear for evaluation. We carry a curated selection of pre-owned equipment and are always interested in quality additions. Pricing is fair and transparent; we'll walk you through the assessment on the spot.

What file format do I need for fine art printing?

We accept JPEG, TIFF, and RAW files. For best results on large-format or fine art prints, shoot in RAW and bring your files on a USB drive or SD card. Our staff will help you choose the right paper type, size, and finish for your image.

Are your passport photos accepted for Canadian government documents?

Yes. Our passport photos meet the standards for Canadian passports, visas, firearms acquisition certificates (FAC/PAL), NEXUS cards, and other government-issued identification. We know the specifications and get them right every time.

Do you offer photography workshops or lessons?

Yes. We periodically run photography workshops for beginners and intermediate shooters, covering composition, exposure, and practical technique around Georgian Bay. Follow us on Instagram (@gabrielephotographycwood) or stop by in-store for the current schedule.
